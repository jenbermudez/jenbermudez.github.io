Best Toys for Girls Ages 6-8

by Penny Williams,
on April 9, 2017

Nowadays, it can be really hard to find an appropriate toy for a young girl. The market is crowded with different toy models and you simply don't know what to choose. That's why, the following article aims to present you the best toys for girls ages 6-8, so you can find the right gift. We hope that our small guide of best toys for girls ages 6-8 will help you in your pursuit for the ideal gift.

Knot-a Quilt

This is a very engaging toy and it's also extremely educational. With it, the girl can build her own quilt, without any needles, stuffing or stitching. It features some squares that can be mixed randomly into a custom pattern. The process is not complicated, but it will develop the girl's creativity and self-esteem. At the end of the game, she will have her own, custom made blanket. The instructions also provide detail info on how to tie a knot, or other common things that might develop her creativity. The materials are durable, and she can use her self-made blanket for a long time.

Karaoke Singing Machine

Every young girl wants to be a famous singer. You can help her with that, by purchasing a nice Karaoke Machine for her. The toy has a working mic, connected to an interactive interface, with speakers and a disco light effects. It also comes with a CD player, on which you can play any of her favorite songs, with or without vocals. You can find plenty of such CD's available online or on the Disney magazines. The toy is great for birthday parties or when she is having fun with her friends. These typically cost less than $40.

Brightlings Talking Plush

This toy is designed as a cute looking figure, which can interact with the user. It can copy whatever the player is saying and it has 100 responses and phrases. You can change her voice by shaking it from side to side. Depending on its position, the talking plush can say things like "I love you!", "How are you today?" or "I'm getting sleepy!". By squeezing Brightlings hand, it will create songs in 6 different genres. In order to play a certain song, you have to hug the doll and it will start playing a musical phrase.

Barbie Careers Doll

Even though Barbie dolls are not as popular as they used to be among young girls, they have to be an important item of their toy collection. A barbie doll is more than a toy and it's actually something that she's going to remember many years from now. This Barbie doll is designed in a modern manner and the character is interested in game developing, having that "career girl" looks. The doll comes with many cute accessories, so the girl won't get bored, playing with it.
