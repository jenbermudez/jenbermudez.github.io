Top 10 Gifts for Kids

by Lincoln Rose,
on April 9, 2017

Choosing a perfect gift for a child is more important than choosing a gift for adults. Children look forward to receiving gifts much more than adults and it can be heartbreaking to see them disappointed when opening your present. Choose one of these ten items to increase your chances of seeing a smile on their faces.

1. Sophie the Giraffe Teether - Vulli

This giraffe is exceptionally flexible, being made of rubber, and has lots of parts such as ears, legs and horns. In addition to the fact that it is soft to touch, and it also has a squeaker to keep the children entertained.

2. Children Einstein Take-along Tunes

An opportunity to acquaint the little one with child-friendly versions of classical music. It is both entertaining and soothing music to your child.

3. Children's First Blocks from Brilliant Basics

Ten brightly hued plastic blocks in a bucket welcome endless sorting, filling and dumping, which also aids eye-hand co-appointment and figuring out how to identify different shapes. The compartment box has a handle which makes it easy to convey.

4. Jacques the Peacock from Lamaze

A beautiful peacock with dazzling colors. It has an easy connection for strollers and carriers. It designed in a way that the little ones will find something interesting to play with.

5. Playschool Busy Ball Popper from Hasbro

The balls scoot to the bottom and fly out the top. It has insane sound effects and 8 bouncy songs which add to the good times.

6. Learn and Groove Musical Table from Leapfrog

Suits ages 6 months and above, it has lots of musical activities that make learning fun. Improves engine skills while they find out about the alphabet, colors, and opposites. The Musical Table offers a jubilee of learning experiences, with sparkling lights and more than 40 songs and melodies.

7. Munchkin Mozart Magic Cube

The Magic Cube provides a great musical establishment for children who loves music

8. Rainforest Jumperoo - Fisher-price

With a rainforest setting, this children jumper stimulates development, with a fun seat and toys in five locations on the stage. There is also an electronic toy on the front which moves and provides music and lights that can be actuated simply by the children. An elephant, monkey, parrot and tiger add to the energy.

9. Children Einstein Animal Exploration Tunnel

Children will love this four-foot-long passage which includes a tweeting red bird, an apple teether, reflect toy and creature photograph cards.

10. Sleep Sheep from Cloud b

The plush Sleep Sheep is cuddly and has five soothing sounds which help to quiet a restless child. These incorporate a heartbeat (like mothers), a calm stream, spring showers, sea waves and harmonious whale sounds.
