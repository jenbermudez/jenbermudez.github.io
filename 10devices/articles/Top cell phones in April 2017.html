Top cell phones in April 2017

by Andre Eogutu,
on April 9, 2017

Here are some of the top cell phones available in April 2017. Each features the latest features and are well worth your interest. Produced by reliable brands, you can invest in one to upgrade your smartphone experience

The Apple iPhone 7 Plus

This is the flagship device from Apple. The iPhone 7 Plus looks similar to its predecessor the iPhone 6s Plus. However, it has a host of brand new technologies inside. Firstly, the camera has been improved such that it has a 12MP sensor on both the back and the front cameras. This results in crisp, clear pictures. Moreover, it has a new Apple A10 Fusion processor. This one is very fast and provides users with great response time. The smartphone is dustproof and completely water resistant. It has an improved battery capacity of 2900mAh to give you more standby and talk time too. The Apple iPhone 7 Plus has a 5.5 inch display, 3GB RAM and a choice between 32, 128 and 256 GB of storage space inside. It runs on the Apple iOS 10.1.

LG G6

The LG G6 is the flagship model from this electronics manufacturer. The phone is a huge improvement from its predecessor, the LG G5. It has a new 5.7 inch Quad HD LCD screen. This one has been tweaked with an aspect ratio of 18:9 and curved corners. To give you fast performance, the LG G6 has a Snapdragon 821 processor, 4 GB of RAM under the hood and 64GB of internal storage. The cellphone has IP68 rating which means that it is water resistant and dustproof. In addition to that, it has two 13MP sensors on its back camera and a 5MP sensor on its front-facing camera. The back of the phone is made of brushed aluminum and this gives it a premium feel.

The HTC 10

The HTC 10 is a superb cellphone available in April 2017. It features an all-metal body. Moreover, it has the latest and most effective hardware under the hood. The cellphone has a fast Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor. Its rear camera has a 12MP sensor and the front-facing one has a 5MP camera sensor. The screen on the HTC 10 is 5.2 inches in size. Moreover, it has a 3,000mAh battery which gives you substantial talk-time. It runs on the Android 6.0.1 operating system.

Google Pixel and Pixel XL

These are two smartphone models released by technology giant, Google. They are high-end smartphones which are extremely capable. The Google Pixel has a 5.0 inch display while the Google Pixel XL has a 5.5 inch display. Both screens feature AMOLED technology. They both come with Google Assistant as a standard feature. Both smartphones have a 4GB RAM. You can then choose between 32GB or 128GB internal memory when making the purchase. They have long lasting batteries too. The Google Pixel has a 2,770 mAh battery while the Google Pixel XL has a 3,450 mAh battery. To keep it running fast, the Google Pixel smartphone have a Snapdragon 821 processor. The back camera has a 12.8MP sensor while the front one has an 8MP sensor for both smartphones. Made of aerospace-grade aluminum, they are well-developed flagship smartphones.
