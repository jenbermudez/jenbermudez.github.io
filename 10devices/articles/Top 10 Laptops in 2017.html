Top 10 Laptops in 2017

by Harper Collins,
on April 9, 2017

Gadgets have turned out to be a basic necessity of people today. There is a large number of people who prefer to work on laptops over tablets. For that type of person, here are 10 of the top laptops of 2017 priced under five hundred dollars.

1. Dell touchscreen laptop

Dell is one of the most reliable brands in the market. The company uses strong materials in its laptop that are capable of tackling daily life. The 15.6 inch screen is bright and makes it easy to work with Windows 10. It possesses one TB of hard disk space, hence you can store your desired movies and songs.

2. HP 15.6 inch laptop

A versatile and dynamic notebook from HP that feels awesome while you are working on it. Its display is comfortable to work with in both in low light and bright light situations. A full charge of battery lasts about seven hours. It is also available with 8GB of RAM; you can work very quickly with this laptop.

3. Asus X555DA laptop

It is made by well known and popular Taiwanese computer hardware an electronics company. This laptop contains a multi-finger facilitated touchpad which makes it easy to use Windows 10. It contains a long lasting battery and IceCool technology that keeps the laptop at a comfortable temperature. The chiclet keyboard let you type accurately and it is hard to resist the touchpad.

4. Toshiba touchscreen laptop

Made by a well-known Japanese brand, it really can be carried anywhere you want to go. The laptop has a lightweight frame and I think it is what makes the device appear sexy. It possesses integrated intel graphics, so if you like playing games, you must purchase this and download some good games.

5. Lenovo laptop

This Lenovo laptop is produced from tough material that keeps it safe from daily wear and tear. The brand is known for producing reliable and durable products. It contains 4 GB RAM so that you can multi-task easily on Windows 10. It has 500 GB of hard drive space, which is plenty for music and videos.

6. Acer Aspire high performance laptop

The laptop appears professional, especially in the black color. It has a comfortable keyboard and touch pad which has multi-finger gestures available. The Acer laptop contains built-in Intel HD graphics, which is particularly useful for gaming and graphics.

7. Asus, F555LA, EH51 laptop

This Asus laptop contains dynamic This Asus laptop contains hardware that makes it capable of handling most software and applications. It has an amazing design and together with its performance, it is one of the best among laptops. It is suitable for both students and business professionals who like to play games and/or work.

8. Lenovo G50-80 laptop

There is nothing to tell about the manufacturing quality, it is completed excellently and the outside body is heat insulating, which maintains the outside of the laptop cool. It possesses an integrated webcam and a loudspeaker to enjoy music and movies. It uses power efficiently and can run about six hours on one full battery charge.

9. Dell convertible two in one laptop

This laptop is light in weight and easily portable. It is 11.6 inches of touch screen that is best suited for daily work. It is available with 128 GB of SSD storage, which is more than suitable for some files and data.

10. Lenovo Ideapad 100 laptop

This is the latest introduction of Lenovo laptop. It has wonderful edges and curves that make the device appear like a premium model. The company has used the best quality materials to create a device that makes it one of the best options.
